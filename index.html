What is elite dangerous?

Elite dangerous is a game that allows for the player base to do anything they wish.

It takes place in space and allows for limitless content. This game is what is called a "sandbox game."

What this means is that the players are able to do anything that they want.

There are numerous activities or careers that are available for players to choose from.

Some of these are:

  • Running a space trucking business.
  • Mining asteroids for minerals and selling them.
  • Pirate activities like killing NPC ships.
  • Running a cruise line to take people to the furthest reaches of the galaxy.

As you can see, there are quite a lot of things that you can do in this game.

Probably the absolute coolest thing in the game is the fact that it is a 1:1 scale representation of our Milky Way Galaxy.

This provides us with a feeling that the space is a big empty void.
